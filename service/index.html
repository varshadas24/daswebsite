Strategy and Service Design

    
        

         Global pandemic has reiterated two philosophies of life - First,
         Change is the only constant (and can come absolutely unannounced). 
         Second, only resilient organizations are capable to navigate 
         sudden market transformations.

         At Entelika, we constantly strive to calibrate how will our client’s 
         business cope with a transformation. As such, Digital Strategy and
         Service Design is an integral function of our service offering. 
         Entelika’s Service Design service involves applying Design Thinking
         methodology into the creation of newer services models for our clients
         - newer business models that are innovative, economic, optimized and 
         deliver more value.

         Our Digital Strategy and Service Design (proprietary) framework allows
         enterprises to mitigate all risks while completely transforming the 
         operating procedure of their core business function(s). We assist with market 
         research, business model framework, digital transformation strategy, 
         organizational change management approach and success measurement frameworks.
